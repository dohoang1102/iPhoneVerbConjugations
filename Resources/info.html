Instructions

Insert an Spanish infinitive verb on the search form and push 'Search'.

The application will show you how this verb is conjugated in Spanish language.

The verbs that you have already ask for them will be cached on the History page so you won't need Internet access to ask for them again.

Dedication

This application is a present for a friend that wants to have the spanish verbs conjugations accessible from his iPhone.

It could be helpful for you also :)
